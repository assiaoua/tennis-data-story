The Map

This map shows the geographical location of all the ATP matches since 2008.

The Players

Each moving circle corresponds to a player, you can follow his journey from cities to cities! Between two matches its position is interpolated on the globe between the start and end position. For player that don't play for a long time, they smoothly dispaear in the middle of the journey and appear again once they are close to the location of their restarting match.

The Tournaments

The orange circle corresponds to the tournaments that hosts the ATP matches, the bigger its size, the more matches the tournaments hosted.

The map is interactive! Hover the players and the tournaments to discover additionnal information. You can also zoom in to have a more precise look at some regions.
